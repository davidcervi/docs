### Schedule Introduction

The [Schedule](https://platform.tutoros.app/schedule) section is where you select which times you are available for students to book
lessons with you.


### Add Availability Slot

To add a new availabilty slot, click on the

If you want a slot to be recurring (as let's say you are free for bookings every Wednesday from 6 pm - 9 pm), then you can tick the `Recurring` checkbox.

When a lesson is booked, that slot is taken in your calendar and no other lessons can be booked for that same time.
