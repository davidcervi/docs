### How to cancel a lesson

To cancel an upcoming lesson, click the ellipsis (three dots) on the top right of the box for the lesson you want to cancel:

![Cancel Lesson 1](/images/cancel-lesson-1.png)

Then click the Cancel option that appears:

![Cancel Lesson 2](/images/cancel-lesson-2.png)

You will then see a final confirmation pop-up to confirm whether or not to cancel the lesson:

![Cancel Lesson 3](/images/cancel-lesson-4.png)

Once the lesson is cancelled, a Cancelled status will be displayed beside the lesson in your lesson list:

![Cancel Lesson 4](/images/cancel-lesson-3.png)
