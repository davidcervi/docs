### Joining A Lesson

Once the time is 5 minutes or less before a lesson start time, you can join the lesson by clicking the `Join Lesson` beside that lesson:

![Joining Lesson](/images/joining-lesson.png)

You will be directed to the [classroom](/lessons/classroom) screen for the lesson and the Zoom call for the lesson will automatically open.

<Note>To join the Zoom call for the lesson, you need to be logged into Zoom with the same email address as your TutorOS account. See the [Zoom Account](/faq/zoom-account) page for more details.</Note>
