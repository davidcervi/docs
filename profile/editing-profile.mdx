## Editing Profile

To edit your profile image and and bio, click on your name on the top right of the toolbar and then click `Profile` in the dropdown:

![Edit Profile 1](/images/edit-profile-1.png)

You can edit the `About Me` and `Profile Image` fields, and then click the `Save button` to save the changes.

![Edit Profile 2](/images/edit-profile-2.png)
