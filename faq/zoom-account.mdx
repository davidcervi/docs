TutorOS uses Zoom to host the video call between you & your student for a lesson!

When joining the Zoom meeting for your lesson, you need to be logged into your Zoom account that uses the same email as your TutorOS account.

If you don't already have a Zoom account, you can can create one for free <a href="https://zoom.us/signup" target="_blank">here</a>.

For the best experience, we recommend using the Zoom app to join the meeting for your lesson. You can download the Zoom app <a href="https://zoom.us/download" target="_blank">here</a>. You can also join Zoom meetings from your web browser if you prefer.
