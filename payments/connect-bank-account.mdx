In order to receive payments for your lessons, you need to first connect your bank account to TutorOS.

### How to Connect Your Bank Account

To connect your bank account, go to the [Billing](https://platform.tutoros.app/billing) section in the app and click the `Connect Bank Account` button:

![Connect Bank Account 1](/images/connect-bank-account-1.png)

You will then see the following pop-up, where you are required to set the country where you are based.  by selecting the country where you are based. This should be the country in which your bank account, used for receiving lesson payments, is located:

![Connect Bank Account 2](/images/connect-bank-account-2.png)
