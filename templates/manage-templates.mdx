### View Your Templates

To view your lesson templates, go to the [Templates](https://platform.tutoros.app/lesson-templates) section in the app:

![Lesson Templates](/images/lesson-templates.png)

To open the action menu for a template, click the gear icon in the top right corner of the template box. This will show the options to edit, archive or delete a template:

![Lesson Template Actions](/images/lesson-templates-action.png)

### Edit Template

To edit a template, click on the `Edit template` link, which will take you to the Edit Template screen. You can edit all settings for a template apart from its currency.

### Archive Template

To archive a template, click on the `Archive template` link. Archiving a template will hide it from your profile page, so students will no longer be able to book lessons for this template.

### Delete Template

To delete a template, click on the `Delete template` link.

<Note>Templates with previously booked lessons can only be archived but not deleted. </Note>
